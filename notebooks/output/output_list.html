Requested Model Outputs#

Output variables are requested in three categories (latter two for LES only):

(1) domain-wide quantities and statistics every 15 minutes
(2) 2D quantities every hour
(3) 3D variables at selected hours for comparison with satellite and ground-based observations

Imports#

import pandas as pd

Read Data from Google Sheets#

All requested output variables are listed in a Google Sheets Document, which is read in below one sheet at a time.
